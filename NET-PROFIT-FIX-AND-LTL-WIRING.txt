TWO issues to fix in this command:

ISSUE 1 — NET PROFIT ACCURACY:
The Net Profit column in the Shipping Center's carrier rate table shows
the SAME dollar value for every carrier row ($79). This is wrong/misleading.

Root causes:
  A) The base object in /api/shipping/center/route.ts does NOT pass
     listingPrice or shippingPreference to the frontend — even though
     Prisma returns them. So the UI falls back to valuationMid (AI
     valuation) which is HIGHER than the actual listing price, producing
     inflated net profit numbers.
  B) Commission rate is hardcoded at 5% — but actual rates vary by tier:
     Free=12%, Starter=8%, Plus=5%, Pro=4%. For now, default to 8%
     (Starter tier — most common at launch) and add a TODO for tier-aware
     calculation later.
  C) The "Best profit" footer ALWAYS subtracts full shipping cost from
     net profit — but the per-row formula only subtracts shipping for
     FREE_SHIPPING and SPLIT_COST (not BUYER_PAYS). These must be
     consistent. Since shipping cost is negotiable between buyer and seller,
     and can be split, the most useful Net Profit should ALWAYS show the
     seller's net AFTER shipping — giving sellers the worst-case/most-
     realistic picture for each carrier option.
  D) The per-row and footer formulas must match.
