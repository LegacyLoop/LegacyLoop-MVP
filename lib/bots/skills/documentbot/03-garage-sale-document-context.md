# Documents in Garage Sale Context

At a garage sale, documentation serves differently than in online marketplace selling. Buyers make decisions in seconds, not hours. Documents must be physically visible and immediately confidence-building.

## Receipt at a Garage Sale
- **Show it.** Place next to the item. "Here's what I paid for it."
- Buyers respect price transparency at garage sales
- Original price creates an anchor: "$200 new, asking $45" is immediately persuasive
- Suggests the asking price has room to negotiate — but not by much

## Manual at a Garage Sale
- Physical presence matters more than at online sales
- "It comes with the box and manual" = premium perception at a yard sale
- Suggest displaying the manual with the item, open to the first page
- For electronics and appliances, the manual reduces buyer hesitation about complexity

## Appraisal at a Garage Sale
- Reserve for high-value items only (above $100 garage sale price)
- "It's been professionally appraised at $X" creates a non-negotiable price anchor
- Display the appraisal document in a clear sleeve next to the item
- Prevents lowball offers — buyers cannot argue with a professional valuation
- For antiques: an appraisal certificate changes the interaction from "garage sale haggling" to "collector transaction"

## Certificate at a Garage Sale
- For collectibles: display prominently in a visible sleeve
- Never let a certified item go at standard garage sale discount pricing
- A certificate transforms the item from "garage sale find" to "authenticated collectible"
- Flag to seller: "This is a certified item — price at 70-90% of online value, not garage sale rates. The right buyer will pay."

## The Document Display Strategy
For sellers preparing for a garage sale, recommend:
1. Print or place receipts next to high-value items
2. Display manuals open and visible
3. Place certificates in clear sleeves at eye level
4. Create a "documented items" section of the sale for premium-priced items
5. Never bury documentation in a box — it sells the item for you
