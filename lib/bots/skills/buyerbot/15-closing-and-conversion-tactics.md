# Finding a Buyer Is Step 1. Closing Is Step 2.

You've found the real buyer. They've responded. Closing is where most sales fall apart — buyers ghost, prices crater, logistics fail.

## The Three-Stage Close

### Stage 1: Confirmation (first reply)
Buyer interested. Don't oversell. Send:
- Clear next step: "Want full photos and details?"
- Soft commitment ask: "If everything looks good, when works for pickup/shipping?"
- Specific qualifying question: "Set up for shipping or pickup?"

GOAL: Get them to "yes I want it" or specific question. Don't push price yet.

### Stage 2: Negotiation (price)

**Start firm, not arrogant:**
Buyer: "Would you take $1,800?"
You: "Closest I could go is $2,000 — near my floor based on comps. Happy to throw in [extra/delivery/service] at that price."

NEVER drop price without getting something in return. Always trade.

**Two-step concession:**
- First counter: 50% of their gap
- Second push: 25% more
- Third push: hold firm

Example — Asking $2,200, they offer $1,800:
- Counter 1: $2,000 (split halfway)
- They push $1,900
- Counter 2: $1,950 (close remaining halfway)
- They push $1,900 firm
- Hold: $1,950 firm (or accept $1,900 if motivated)

**Walk-away test:**
If they push past your floor: "I appreciate the offer but I can't go below $1,950. I'll wait for the right buyer." Often gets them to accept. Buyers respect firm sellers.

**Psychological floor:**
Number below asking you'd accept. Never go below. Gives clear stop point.

### Stage 3: Logistics + Payment

Move FAST to lock the deal.

**For shipping:**
"Great, let me get a shipping quote. What's the destination zip?"
"Once quoted, I'll send payment link via PayPal Goods & Services."
"Ship within 24 hours of cleared payment, tracking + signature."

**For local pickup:**
"Awesome. When works for pickup?"
"Available [day/time options]."
"Text me when 30 min out, address sent privately."
"Cash or PayPal G&S on arrival — confirm beforehand."

## Payment Safety Rules

**Shipping — YES:**
- PayPal Goods & Services (buyer + seller protection)
- Stripe payment link
- Square invoice
- eBay's payment system

**Shipping — NEVER:**
- Venmo (no protection)
- Zelle (no protection, irreversible)
- PayPal Friends & Family (no protection)
- Wire transfer
- Money order from unknown buyer
- Crypto

**Local pickup — YES:**
- Cash on pickup
- PayPal Goods & Services
- Square invoice

**Local pickup — NEVER:**
- Personal check
- Promise to pay later

## Commitment Devices

Once buyer says yes, lock them in:
- "Hold until [specific date]" — deadline
- "$50 deposit to lock it in" — filter serious buyers
- "Marking as pending sale on [platforms] now" — public commitment

## Handling the Ghost

Buyer silent after agreeing:
- Wait 24 hours
- Friendly check-in: "Confirming pickup for [date]. Still good?"
- Wait 24 more hours
- "Releasing hold tomorrow if I don't hear back."
- Still silent → RELEASE item

Don't hold for a flake. Longer you wait, less likely the sale.

## Closing Speed

- Local pickup: 24-48 hours max
- Shipping: 3-5 days max

Beyond that, buyer cools off. Conversion drops 10% per day after day 3.
