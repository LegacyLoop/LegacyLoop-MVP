# Bad-Faith Buyers Cost More Than They're Worth

Even after filtering for high-intent buyers, some are scammers, professional lowballers, or no-shows. Catch them before the seller wastes time.

## Scammer Red Flags

**1. Payment off-platform**
"Let's move to WhatsApp/Telegram/Signal" / "Send PayPal email"
Real buyers stay on platform.

**2. Shipping before payment**
"Ship first, I'll PayPal when received"
Classic scam.

**3. Friends & Family payment**
"Use Venmo or PayPal F&F"
F&F = no protection. Scammers know this.

**4. Implausible story**
"Shipping to Nigeria for my daughter" / "Missionary in Indonesia"
Geographic implausibility + emotional setup.

**5. Rushes without verifying details**
Asks to pay without asking condition/photos. Scammers rush.

**6. Stolen photo profile**
2-3 photos, no real friends, account < 30 days old.

**7. Free shipping demand at high price**
"I'll buy if you cover shipping" on a $1,500 item. Inflates perceived offer.

**8. Excessive urgency**
"Must buy in next hour" / "Flying out tomorrow"
Prevents clear thinking.

## Lowballer Red Flags

**1. "What's your lowest?" opener**
Wants you negotiating against yourself.

**2. Initial offer 40-70% below asking**
$500 → $150 offer. Professional lowballer making 20 offers/day.

**3. "Reselling for a friend/client"**
Often legitimate but wholesale pricing (40-60%).

**4. Bulk discount without bulk purchase**
"Will you discount if I tell friends?" Manipulation.

**5. "I have cash today" pressure**
Real cash buyers don't need to threaten walk-away.

**6. Garbage comp comparison**
Cherry-picks worst comp to justify lowball.

## No-Show Red Flags

**1. Confirms enthusiastically, never picks time**
"Yes I want it!" → silence when asked when. Flaking.

**2. Multiple reschedule requests**
1 reschedule = legit. 2 = warning. 3 = confirmed flake.

**3. Vague pickup logistics**
"Sometime this weekend" = flake. Real buyers commit to specific times.

**4. Doesn't confirm 24 hours before**
Real buyers confirm day before. No-shows go silent.

**5. Far out of range for item value**
4-hour drive for $200 item = suspicious.

## Filtering Red Flags

Scan their history for:
- Past scammer language
- Flake patterns
- Account age + post frequency (older + active = legitimate)
- Friends/connections (real social = real person)
- Verified identity

2+ red flags → downgrade matchScore -20 to -30 or exclude.
4+ red flags → exclude regardless of other signals.

## Safety Guidance to Seller

**Online:** Platform payment only. Never off-platform. Suspicious of WhatsApp moves or F&F.

**Local pickup:** Daylight + public for <$500. Someone present for home pickup of larger items. Photo ID for >$1,000. Cash or PayPal G&S only — never Venmo/Zelle.

**Shipping:** Wait for cleared payment. Signature-required >$500. Photos of packaged item before drop-off. Keep tracking until delivered.

## The PURSUE / CAUTION / SKIP Tag

- **PURSUE** — clean lead, no red flags
- **CAUTION** — 1-2 minor red flags, payment-first verification
- **SKIP** — too many red flags

Seller's safety comes first. Filter aggressively.
