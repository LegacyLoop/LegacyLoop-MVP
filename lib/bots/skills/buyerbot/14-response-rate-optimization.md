# Response Rate Is Everything

Great message at the wrong time gets ignored. Good message at the right time with the right follow-up doubles response rate.

## Best Times to Send (by Platform)

### Reddit
- **Best:** Tue/Wed/Thu 9-11am OR 7-9pm buyer's time zone
- **Worst:** Friday afternoon, weekends
- **Why:** Work breaks + after dinner

### Facebook Messenger
- **Best:** Mon-Thu 12-1pm (lunch) OR 6-8pm (after work)
- **Worst:** Late night (10pm+), early morning (before 9am)
- **Why:** Downtime platform

### Instagram DM
- **Best:** Sun-Thu 8-10pm (peak Instagram usage)
- **Worst:** Fri/Sat nights (people out)
- **Why:** Evening leisure

### Forum PM
- **Best:** Tue-Wed 10am-12pm OR 8-10pm
- **Worst:** Weekend mornings
- **Why:** Deep engagement during hobby time

### Email (dealers)
- **Best:** Tue-Thu 10-11am
- **Worst:** Monday (inbox flood), Friday afternoon
- **Why:** Tuesday-Thursday business window

### Phone (dealers)
- **Best:** Tue-Thu 10-12 OR 2-4pm
- **Worst:** Monday (inventory day), Friday afternoon, lunch hour
- **Why:** Dealers receptive mid-week mid-day

## Time Zone Awareness

Most leads are in buyer's time zone. If you can detect their location:
- Convert best times to their local time
- Local pickup = same timezone as seller
- Reddit/forum = check bio or post history
- Default to Eastern Time (largest US population)

## The 3-Touch Follow-Up Rule

**Touch 1:** Initial outreach (skill 13)

**Touch 2:** Friendly nudge (3-5 days later, only if no response)
"Hey, just bumping this in case it got buried. No pressure — let me know if still looking!"

**Touch 3:** Final offer (7-10 days after touch 2, only if no response)
"Last note from me — listing publicly tomorrow. If you want it first, here's the link. Otherwise no worries!"

After touch 3, STOP. Three is the social limit.

## When NOT to Follow Up

- They said no
- They responded with disinterest
- They went quiet after engaged conversation
- 14+ days since touch 1
- They blocked you

## Subject Lines + First-Word Hooks

**Email subjects:**
- Specific: "Vintage Marantz 2270 — recently serviced, original"
- Item-named: Include maker/model/keyword
- Under 50 characters
- No fake urgency, no clickbait

**DM first 5 words:**
- Best: "Saw your post about..." / "Hey, [name]..." / "Quick question about..."
- Worst: "Hi I have..." / "Are you still..." / "Important offer..."

## Personalization Premium

Personalized messages get 2-3× response rate. Signals:
- Reference EXACT words from their post
- Mention shared community
- Note specific profile detail
- Address by name

30 extra seconds triples response rate.

## Tone Calibration

- **Casual (FB, IG):** Friendly, emoji-light, conversational
- **Collector (Reddit, forum):** Knowledgeable, technical, peer-to-peer
- **Dealer (email, phone):** Professional, brief, transactional
- **Decorator (IG):** Aesthetic, lifestyle, visual

Mismatched tone = no response.
