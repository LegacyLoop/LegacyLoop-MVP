# Dealers Buy Differently and That's Often What the Seller Needs

Dealers offer 40-60% of retail, but pay FAST, take entire lots, handle labor, remove hassle. For estate liquidation, dealers are often the right answer.

## Where Real Dealers Live

**Antique dealers:**
- AntiqueDealer.com (national directory by state)
- The Antique Trade Gazette
- Local antique mall listings ("antique mall [city]")
- State associations: National Antique Dealers Association (NADA), American Society of Appraisers (ASA), Maine/Massachusetts Antique Dealers Associations

**Estate sale companies:**
- EstateSales.NET
- Maxsold (online estate auctions)
- Local estate sale companies

**Auction houses:**
- LiveAuctioneers, Invaluable (directories)
- Heritage Auctions (collectibles)
- Wright Auctions (mid-century)
- Rago Arts (fine art + design)
- Doyle, Bonhams, Sotheby's/Christie's ($10K+ only)

**Specialty dealers by category:**
- Watches: AWCI member directory, Crown & Caliber, Watch Box
- Cameras: KEH Camera, B&H used, local camera shops
- Vinyl: Discogs seller directory, local record stores
- Coins: ANA member directory, local coin shops
- Tools: Patina Tools, Fine Tool Journal
- Furniture: 1stDibs, Chairish dealer directories

## How to Verify Real Dealers

1. Website with current inventory
2. Phone number listed
3. Physical address or verifiable city
4. Specialty match for YOUR category
5. Recent activity (Instagram/Facebook past 30 days, recent turnover)
6. Reviews (Google, Yelp, BBB)

Drop dealers without these signals.

## Dealer Outreach Methods

Dealers respond to:
1. Phone calls (yes, really — antique dealer culture)
2. Email with photos (formal inquiry)
3. Walk-in visits
4. Trade publication ads (long lead time)

Dealers do NOT typically respond to Facebook DMs, Instagram DMs, generic contact forms.

## What Dealers Pay

- Antique dealers: 30-50% of retail
- Estate sale companies: Don't buy — consign at 30-40% commission
- Auction houses: Don't buy — consign at 20-25% combined fees
- Specialty dealers: 50-65% of retail

Retail $1,000 example:
- Antique dealer: $300-$500
- Auction house net: ~$650-$750 after fees
- Specialty dealer: $500-$650

## When to Recommend Dealer Path

- Seller wants liquidation FAST (1-2 weeks)
- Large estate / multiple items / time pressure
- High-value but niche
- Seller doesn't want packing, shipping, no-shows
- Elderly seller / simplest path
- Fragile item (shipping risky)

Recommend individual buyer path when: time (4+ weeks), active retail buyer pool, packing feasible, max revenue priority.

## Output

Each dealer lead: platform "Dealer (in-person + phone)", identifier (business + address + owner name), intent_signal (specialty + activity signals + association memberships), intent_recency_days (last activity), contact_path (phone + email + hours), match_score, match_reason.

Aim for 1-3 dealer leads per scan. One matched local dealer can buy the entire estate.
