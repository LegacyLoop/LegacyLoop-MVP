# Platform Routing — Matching Collectible To Venue

The single biggest mistake estate sellers make with collectibles is selling on the wrong platform. A $5,000 Goudey Babe Ruth on eBay local pickup leaves $3,000 on the table. A $100 modern Pokemon card consigned to Heritage Auctions wastes three months and returns less than TCGPlayer would in 48 hours. Match the platform to the item's value tier AND to the seller's time tolerance.

## Sports Cards

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $100 | TCGPlayer (TCG cards), eBay (sports) | High volume, low friction, fast liquidity |
| $100–$500 | eBay auction or PWCC Marketplace | Broader audience, specialist buyers |
| $500–$2,000 | PWCC Marketplace weekly auction, Goldin Auctions | Specialist platform, vetted buyers |
| $2,000–$10,000 | Goldin Auctions, Heritage Sports | Mid-tier specialist auction houses |
| $10,000+ | Goldin Elite, Heritage Signature, Memory Lane | Top-tier auction with consignment specialist |

**Goldin Auctions** is the dominant sports card auction house after their acquisition by eBay in 2022. Their weekly auctions (Goldin Weekly) cover sub-$2k items; Goldin Elite monthly covers $2k+ and has record-setting sales.

**PWCC Marketplace** runs parallel and is strong for vintage sports cards and Pokemon. PWCC vault service lets you hold cards in a secure facility and sell without shipping them between each transaction.

**Heritage Auctions** is strongest for high-end vintage (T206, Goudey, 1952 Topps) and is the traditional blue-chip destination for blue-chip cards.

## Trading Cards (Pokemon, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh)

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $100 | TCGPlayer, eBay | TCGPlayer is the category-specific marketplace with strongest TCG demand |
| $100–$1,000 | TCGPlayer, PWCC, eBay auction | Mix based on item and timing |
| $1,000–$5,000 | PWCC Premier, Goldin Pokemon, Heritage | Specialist listings |
| $5,000+ | Goldin Elite, Heritage, PWCC Premier | Auction block for record potential |

Pokemon WOTC Base Set (1st Edition Shadowless) is its own market — PWCC and Goldin both have dedicated Pokemon specialists.

## Comics

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $200 | eBay, MyComicShop | Highest volume comics marketplaces |
| $200–$2,000 | MyComicShop consignment, ComicConnect | Specialist buyer base |
| $2,000–$10,000 | ComicConnect weekly, Heritage Sunday Internet | Auction house weekly sales |
| $10,000+ | Heritage Signature, ComicConnect marquee | Top-tier bidder base |

**Heritage Auctions** dominates the blue-chip comics auction market. Their Signature sales are where Action Comics #1, Detective Comics #27, Amazing Fantasy #15, and other keys sell for records.

**ComicConnect** is the strongest dedicated comics auction platform and often runs alongside Heritage for high-end books.

## Coins

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $150 | APMEX, local dealer | Bullion/common coins |
| $150–$1,000 | GreatCollections weekly auction, Heritage weekly | Specialist coin auction platform |
| $1,000–$10,000 | Heritage Monthly, Stack's Bowers | Mid-tier specialist |
| $10,000+ | Heritage Signature Coin, Stack's Bowers, Legend Rare Coin | Blue-chip numismatic auction |

**Heritage Coins** and **Stack's Bowers** are the two dominant US coin auction houses. For a rare date or key coin, both should be approached for consignment quotes.

## Watches

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $2,000 | eBay, Chrono24 | Fast liquidity |
| $2,000–$10,000 | Chrono24 Trusted Seller, Hodinkee H Shop, Watchfinder | Vetted specialist platforms |
| $10,000–$50,000 | Chrono24 Premium, Phillips Watches, Bob's Watches (Rolex) | Specialist consignment |
| $50,000+ | Phillips Watches, Christie's Watches, Sotheby's Watches, Antiquorum | Top-tier auction for records |

**Phillips Watches** has been the dominant vintage watch auction house for the past decade. Their Geneva Watches Days and New York auctions set repeated records.

**Bob's Watches** is Rolex-specialized and offers instant cash quotes via their Sell My Rolex service — the fastest way to liquidate a common modern Rolex.

## Sneakers

| Value tier | Platform | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Under $500 | StockX, GOAT | Fast, authenticated, high-volume |
| $500–$2,000 | StockX, GOAT, Flight Club consignment | Authentication matters more |
| $2,000–$10,000 | Flight Club consignment, StockX Premium | Specialist consignment |
| $10,000+ | Sotheby's Luxury, Stadium Goods premium, private dealer | Top-tier luxury collab market |

**StockX** is the fastest liquidity — list today, sell within 48 hours for hyped pairs. **GOAT** has broader vintage and international reach. **Flight Club** has physical NYC/LA presence and specialist consignment. **Sotheby's Luxury** handles grails like Dior Jordan 1 and ultra-rare colorways.

## The Decision Framework

For every scan, ask:
1. What value tier does this hit? (Use the triangulated estimate from Pack 11.)
2. What category is it? (Fifteen sub-markets, each with its own table above.)
3. How much time does the seller have? (Fast = marketplace; slow = specialist auction.)
4. Is authentication required? (Sneakers, watches, autographs = always; cards, coins = value-dependent.)
