Carrier rate quotes expire. Typical carrier rate
validity is 24 hours. The Shipping Center needs
to track this and alert users.

1. EXPIRATION STATUS on every saved quote:
   Based on savedAt timestamp:
   — FRESH (< 12 hours): green badge "✓ Fresh"
   — EXPIRING SOON (12-24 hours): amber badge
     "⚠️ Expiring soon — refresh before shipping"
   — EXPIRED (> 24 hours): red badge
     "❌ Expired — rates may have changed"
     [🔄 Refresh Rates] button prominent

2. EXPIRATION ALERT BANNER:
   At the top of the Shipping Center (above tabs or
   in the intelligence strip area), if ANY items have
   expiring or expired quotes, show:

   IF expiring (12-24hrs):
   "⚠️ [X] quote(s) expiring soon — refresh before
    rates change"
   Amber tinted banner. Clickable — highlights the
   items with expiring quotes.

   IF expired (>24hrs):
   "❌ [X] quote(s) expired — click to refresh rates"
   Red tinted banner. Clickable.

3. AUTOMATIC EXPIRATION CHECK:
   On component mount AND on each auto-refresh (30s),
   scan all items with saved quotes. Check the savedAt
   timestamp. Update the Fresh/Expiring/Expired status.

   This is a CLIENT-SIDE calculation — just compare
   savedAt to Date.now(). No API call needed.

4. QUOTE COUNTDOWN on expanded quote details:
   When viewing a quote's details, show:
   "Quote valid for approximately [X] hours"
   Or "Quote expired [X] hours ago"
   Use a subtle countdown style — not alarming,
   just informational.
